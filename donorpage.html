Welcome, dear donor! Your health is important to us. Please take note of the following health advisories before donating blood:

Healthy Tips

  • Blood Donation can help you in reducing blood pressure
  • There are lower risk for having heart attacks
  • Since the blood will regenerate and refresh, therefore viscosity of the blood reduces associated with the formation of blood clots, heart attacks, and stroke.

One Blood Donation can save up to three lives

"Creating moments of kindness during a time of need does wonders for your mental health"

Some Minimum Health requirements to be a healthy donor:

  • Minimum Height: 150 cm (59 inches)
  • Minimum Weight: 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Blood Pressure: Should be within the normal range (consult a healthcare professional if uncertain)
  • Blood Sugar Levels: Should be within the normal range (consult a healthcare professional if uncertain)
  • Hemoglobin Level: Should be at least 12.5 g/dL (for females) or 13.0 g/dL (for males)
  • Minimum Gap Between Donations: At least 3 months

If you meet these criteria and are in good health, you are eligible to donate blood.

You may be required to wait for a few months if you have undergone any vaccinations.

You cannot donate blood if you have undergone tattoos or piercings recently. You need to wait for at least 3 months for your and others' health precautions.
